# CrazyGames Trend Research

**Last updated:** 2026-06-05

---

## Platform Snapshot (2025–2026)

- **40% growth** in CrazyGames popularity vs 2024
- **Hypercasual + puzzle = 99%** of all tracked browser gaming playtime
- Hybrid-casual (hypercasual + progression system) is the emerging sweet spot
- **Shell Shockers** = one of most-played games — IO-style multiplayer remains king
- Mobile traffic is dominant — always design mobile-first

## What Gets Featured on CrazyGames (2025 patterns)

1. **Unique thumbnail** — bright, high-contrast, shows the mechanic immediately
2. **Mobile portrait orientation** — 65%+ traffic is mobile
3. **Under 10-second comprehension** — if it needs a tutorial it won't get featured
4. **Satisfying core loop** — the action itself feels good even before progression
5. **Progression hook** — level numbers, score milestones, something to chase
